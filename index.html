Segments Explorer

Use Segments Explorer and become a segments conqueror.

If you love this project, please give me a star here.

I have heard many cyclists complain that Strava's segment-finding feature is really hard to use,
so I developed this website to help you find the segments you like.

New feature: If you're having trouble making a decision, you can press the R key or the button in the bottom-right corner,
and Segments Explorer will randomly pick a segment for you.

Go and conquer the segments. Good luck and have fun.

If the segment you are looking for is not listed, feel free to contact me.
